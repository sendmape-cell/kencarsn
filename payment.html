Payment Methods

Which payment methods are accepted?

Orders are currently paid through PayPal invoice. Depending on PayPal availability, customers may pay with PayPal balance, card, or other payment options shown by PayPal.

Is my payment information secure?

Payment details are processed by PayPal. Yirtuamlak does not store credit card or bank details on this website.

How does PayPal payment work?

When you press Add to Cart or Buy Now, the PayPal invoice opens in a new tab. Complete payment there, then send your size and shipping details so the order can be processed manually.

Where can I enter a discount code?

Discounts are handled manually. If you have a discount code, send it together with your order details after payment.

When will I be charged?

The amount is charged when you complete the PayPal invoice payment.
