Service & Help

Order support

For order questions, payment confirmation, sizing, delivery, or address updates, contact Yirtuamlak support directly after placing your order.

What should I send after payment?

  • Selected size: S, M, L, XL, 2XL, or 3XL.
  • Full name.
  • Shipping address.
  • Email and phone number for delivery if required.

Can I change my size?

Size changes are possible only before the order is submitted to fulfillment. Message as soon as possible.

How do I know my order is confirmed?

Your order is confirmed after PayPal payment is received and your size and shipping details are provided.

Contact

Use the artist/store contact channel shared with your order or reach out through the official social links.
